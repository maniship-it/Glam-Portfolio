# Brand Style Guide: Puja Glam Makeup Studio

## Typography
- **Headings (H1-H6):** `Montserrat` - Conveys elegance, modernity, and a premium editorial feel.
- **Body Text:** `Inter` - Highly legible, neutral, and clean for paragraphs and UI elements.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Background:** Deep Charcoal (`#1a1a1a` to `#121212`) - Provides a luxurious, high-contrast canvas that makes images pop.
- **Accent:** Maroon (`#7b1f32`) - Used for subtle highlights, active states, and emotional warmth.
- **Secondary/Highlights:** Warm Beige / Gold (`#d8c3a5` or similar) - Used for primary actions, borders, and glowing focus states to emphasize the "Glam" aspect.
- **Text:** Soft White / Off-White (`#f5f5f5`) - For readability against the deep charcoal background.

## Spacing Scale
- Utilizes standard Tailwind spacing (4px base).
- **Sections:** `py-24` (96px) for generous breathing room between major content blocks.
- **Cards/Containers:** `p-8` (32px) for internal padding of service and testimonial cards.

## UI Elements
- **Borders:** Thin, semi-transparent white/beige lines (`border-white/10`).
- **Hover States:** Subtle upward translation (`-translate-y-2`), increased glow/shadow, and background brightness.
- **Focus States:** Distinct golden-beige outline for accessibility (`focus-visible:ring-primary focus-visible:ring-offset-2`).
- **Corners:** Rounded-2xl (`1rem`) for soft, approachable luxury.
